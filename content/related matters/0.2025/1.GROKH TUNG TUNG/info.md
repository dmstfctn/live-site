Exhibited: Edinburgh Art Festival<span class="dc-hide-on-large"><br><br></span>

Two machine learning agents tell each other jokes about real world examples of cheating AIs. They speak in an imitational language inspired by 16<sup>th</sup> century Grammelot and composed entirely of the mistaken outputs of a text to speech AI model. Generated in response to the artists’ ambiguous prompting technique, the language includes hallucinations, screams, laughter, musical snippets, and hesitations.

Part paper cutouts, part silhouettes, the characters resemble humanoids and were inspired by 20th shadow theatre of German filmmaker Lotte Reiniger, theatrical costumes work by Picasso and the British puppet theatre tradition of Punch and Judy.
